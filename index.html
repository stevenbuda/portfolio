Steven Buda

Designer & Photographer

I’m a first-year Master’s student at the University of Michigan studying User Experience Design. I have a passion for human-centered design, photography, fitness, and foood! I love creating in a space where design, technology, humans and business intersect.

I believe in technology that empowers, not detracts, the holistic experience. My interests are in Product Design, UX Writing, and Digital Strategy. I hope to lead design and content teams in the future, as well as dabble in speaking at design conferences. I personally believe that great design leaders are ones that take the time to create a responsible and inclusive design culture.

In my free time I enjoy urban exploring, hanging in hip coffee shops, backpacking, and staying up to date on the latest fashion and design trends!

Thank you for taking the time to get to know a little bit about me and my projects. Always happy to chat more!
